Place your hero banner image here.

Filename: hero.jpg  (JPG or PNG, recommended 1400×500px minimum)

How to use:
  1. Drop your photo here as "hero.jpg"
  2. Commit and push to GitHub — Vercel will auto-deploy in ~20 seconds

Google Drive alternative:
  If you prefer to host on Google Drive, open index.html and replace:
    src="img/hero.jpg"
  with your Google Drive direct-download link:
    src="https://drive.google.com/uc?export=view&id=YOUR_FILE_ID"

  To get the FILE_ID: open the image in Drive → Share → Copy link
  The link looks like: https://drive.google.com/file/d/FILE_ID/view
  Extract only the FILE_ID part and paste it above.
